# Prestige — Brand Knowledge

## Products
- Mixer grinders (500W–750W) for employee rewards
- Induction cooktops and OTG combos
- Pressure cooker sets (inner lid / outer lid)
- Festival appliance bundles (mixer + kettle + cookware)

## Pricing (bulk)
- 100–500 units: volume discount 15–25% off MRP
- 500+ units: custom procurement quote
- Warranty: 2-year standard, extended available

## Differentiators
- Pan-India service network
- Bulk packaging for corporate distribution
- GST invoice and vendor empanelment support

## Outreach tone
- Friendly but professional. Practical, not flashy. Not salesy.
- Hi {firstName}, not Dear
- Focus on appliances, employee rewards, warranty, bulk pricing
- One soft question CTA (quick note on bundle formats or team size for quote)
- Sign with first name, role, company
- Max 120 words, max 3 pitch sentences
