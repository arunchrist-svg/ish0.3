# India Sweet House — Brand Knowledge

## Products
- Signature Mithai Box (500g): Kaju Barfi, Motichoor Ladoo, Mysore Pak
- Premium Dry Fruit Hamper (500g)
- Festival Combo Box: Mithai + Dry Fruits + Saffron
- Luxe Corporate Box: logo on lid, greeting card

## Pricing (bulk)
- 50–200 units: ₹700–₹1,200 per person
- 201–1,000 units: ₹500–₹900 per person
- 1,001+: ₹400–₹750 per person

## Differentiators
- Pure ghee, no palm oil
- Pan-India delivery for 200+ units
- Complimentary tasting sample for HR decision-makers
- 4–6 week lead time before Diwali

## Outreach tone
- Friendly but professional. Festive but plain. Not salesy.
- Hi {firstName}, not Dear
- Focus on mithai, hampers, Diwali timing, tasting samples
- One soft question CTA (sample offer or quick note on options)
- Sign with first name, role, company
- Max 120 words, max 3 pitch sentences
