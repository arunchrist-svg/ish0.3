# Campaign: Mass Ordering

## Focus
- Volume pricing and procurement timelines
- Minimum order quantities and GST invoicing
- Delivery logistics for multi-location companies

## CTA patterns
- Ask for approximate unit count and delivery locations
- Offer procurement call or formal quote
- Mention vendor empanelment if relevant

## Avoid
- Seasonal-only language (works year-round)
- Overly casual tone with procurement buyers
